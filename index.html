Welcome

Welcome to StickyNotes by Nazir—a personal space dedicated to science, observation, exploration, and lifelong learning. Here I document my research journey, scientific insights, travel experiences, photography, and reflections inspired by the fascinating patterns found in both nature and science.

About Me

Science begins with observation. I am a PhD researcher exploring antimicrobial resistance, natural products, essential oils, metabolomics, nanotechnology, and computational biology. My work focuses on understanding complex biological systems and developing innovative, sustainable solutions to global health challenges.

Beyond research, I am passionate about travel, photography, and discovering patterns hidden within both nature and science. Whether observing microbial life through a microscope, analyzing scientific data, or exploring unfamiliar landscapes, I am driven by curiosity, critical thinking, and the pursuit of lifelong learning.

I believe that meaningful discoveries emerge from careful observation, thoughtful questioning, and the ability to connect ideas across disciplines and experiences.

— Nazir S.K.
